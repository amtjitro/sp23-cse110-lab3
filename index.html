Brainstorm

We want to decide on a workflow and organize when we need to start things by. We suggested a brainstorm period where we discuss what we want to implement and how. This also includes consulting other users/people to gather data on what they want to see. Then we will move into the programming part once we have all the logistics and ideas sorted out. Once we have the basics, we can start discussing additional features and adding in what others said they might want to see. So, our process will look like:

  1. Discuss basic features and the goal of this project
  2. Gather preferences from people who might be using this
  3. Program the bare basics & basic functionality
  4. Add in additional features that were discussed early
  5. Regroup and discuss
  6. Repeat 4 & 5 as many times as necessary

Anthony specializes in regional cuisines and was thinking of making a weekly spotlight on different dishes such as ratatoullie or foie gras. Ina will support on this feature and Anthony will lead.

Bobby wants to advertise all of their shows and websites. Wants to create an author/developer section which includes everyone's biographies and links to their work/shows. Bobby will lead on this feature and Alex will support.
